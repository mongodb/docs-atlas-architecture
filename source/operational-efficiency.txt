======================
Operational Efficiency
======================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

The Operational Efficiency pillar of the {+service+} {+waf+} includes
features that support your organization's operations with minimum
expenditure of time and effort, including tools for automation
and monitoring and alert tools and integrations.

Use the following {+atlas-arch-center+} resources to learn about operational efficiency in {+service+}:

.. card-group::
   :columns: 2
   :style: extra-compact

   .. card::
      :headline: Automation
      :url: https://mongodb.com/docs/manual/data-modeling/
      :icon: atlas_search
      :icon-alt: Data modeling icon

      Automate resource deployment by integrating with Infrastructure as Code (IaC) tools that templatize your processes.

   .. card::
      :headline: Monitoring and Alerts
      :url: https://mongodb.com/docs/atlas/setup-cluster-security/
      :icon: atlas_search
      :icon-alt: Atlas security icon

      Monitor system performance in real time, detect issues, and integrate with your third-party observability and alerting stack.
